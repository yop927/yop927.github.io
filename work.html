NARA (National Archives and Records Administration (June ~ August, 2016)

-Worked on a team of 4, and there were 3 teams in total. Each team was required to read certain number of box
that contains multiple files that were written during the World War 2. The documents were about the battle between
the U.S. and Japan on Pacific Islands. Our job was to take pictures if we find any information about the soldiers,
then sorted the information based on the battlefield location, grid number or name of the troop on the Excel sheet.
